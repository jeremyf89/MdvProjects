Project 1

Obsession with Pizza

 

My family loves pizza, but my son’s hunger for it is absurd. His name is Anthony, and it’s obvious that he loves Meatos pizza the most. Every time we visit Meatos he goes all out, last time we visited he must have eaten 10 pieces. So he’s going to be happy that Meatos pizza is our eating destination tonight! It’s not cheap so, I’ve decided that if he wants at least 12 pieces ill buy him a buffet. He has to finish his chores though, or he won’t be eating a buffet tonight, rather ill just buy him a few slices, and to him that should be punishment enough.

.

I chose Obsession with Pizza simply, because my family does have a strong taste for it. My sons liking of pizza is crazy at time, and for the record Meatos pizza is fictional place. .

1/11/13, Jeremy Farris
